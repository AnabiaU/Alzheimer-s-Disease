Alzheimer's Disease

What are the Causes?

The causes are not fully understood, but it might be because of these factors:
-Genetics: Having a family history of Alzheimer’s does not mean you will have it for sure, but you are likely to develop it.
-Age: Alzheimer’s isn’t a part of aging, but as you grow older, the chances increase.
-Head Injury: Several studies have shown that people who have suffered a traumatic brain injury have higher risks or Alzheimer’s disease.
