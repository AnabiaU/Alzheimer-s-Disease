Alzheimer's Disease

What are some impacts?

-Loss of Independence: This refers to not being able to do daily life activities by themselves. This includes using the toilet, dressing, bathing, eating, etc.
-Communication difficulties: People with Alzheimer’s have difficulty communicating with others. They struggle finding words and expressing themselves.
-Impact on Relationships: Alzheimer’s can strain a person’s relationships with others, especially when they no longer recognize others and become aggressive towards them.
